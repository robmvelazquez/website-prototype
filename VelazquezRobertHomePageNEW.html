Spooky Series Bumpers

Tired of that old factory plastic bumper? Or maybe you're looking to replace an old bumper that has seen more trails than your every-day mall-crawler? Well what ever the reason is - our new Spooky Series Bumpers have you covered. Using our state-of-the-art CNC machining as well as our highly trained and extremely talented master welders, we can guarantee our product will protect whatever the trail may throw at you. No matter which one of our beautiful hand-crafted bumpers you decide to choose from, each one has the option of alluminum or steel. If you prefer protection and don't mind a little extra weight, choose our 3/16 laser cut steel.. If you care more about weight reduction and performance, choose our 5/16 inch raw alluminum.

Adventure Series Suspension System

Introducing our newest addition to our lineup of high-quality parts, our team of highly talented mechanicl engineers have partnered with some of the greatest Jeep'ers ever to roam the trails to present to you our newest development - our Adventure Series Suspension System!

  • Shocks
    - Engineered to handle the rough terrain as well as the tight cornering and speed that comes with high speed performance. These shock absorbers improve your Wrangler's heat dissipation and immensely improve the handling of your vehicle, what ever the occassion is.
  • Coil Springs
    - Increase your rig's ride height while maintaining an outstanding ride quality with the addition of our Adventure Series Coil Springs. Be sure to stop by and browse our choices of control arms! Control arms will alleviate any geometric suspension concerns following the install of our Adventure series coil springs by providing better clearance, dampening, and overall drivability.
  • Control Arms
    - Adding additional lift to your rig is just the beginning of modifying for the average Jeep'er. What most do not realize is that suspension systems involve a massive amount of geometric tinkering to achieve the maximum full range of motion to accomodate your newly installed lift-kit. The answer to improving your rig's flex and suspension geometry is our newest set of Adventure Series adjustable control arms. These control arms will provide better clearance, dampening, and overall drivability.
  • Sway Bar
    - Get rid of that old factory sway bar and add our high quality Adventure Series Sway Bar to evenly distribute weight without compromising traction off-road. Crafted from 1-Inch diameter steel and 1/2 inch steel sway bar links, have confidence our sway bar system will provide the ultimate articulation and smooth performance - whether on the street or on the trail!
